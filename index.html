Hi, I am Tania Sanchez

UX Researcher & Anthropologist

I analyze anecdotal and emotional meanings to identify people’s necessities to achieve novel insights and understand how to solve specific problems.

My humanistic nature led me to study and teach Anthropology. Since 2005, I have researched various groups of people, and through my work I have developed and enhanced my empathy to gain a deep understanding of the socio-cultural context mainly focus on needs and feelings of people. Studying national and international cultural mileaux has given me a keen sense about what matters to others, the facility to think creatively and develop effective solutions to specific problems.

Design is facinating to me because it influences the outcomes of countless daily routines and allows designers to impact their material surroundings. Doing applied anthropology for the design field has let me approach user’s needs and constraints and recognize how important is that users take an active part in the design process. So, my role is to serve as a mediator between the designer and the user in the design process.
